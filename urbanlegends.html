Urban Legends

From Bigfoot to Nessie, urban legends have never ceased to capture the imagination. Check out the creepy urban legends contained in the library below:

--------------------------

Film: The Mothman Prophecies, 2002
After finding out that a Moth-like figure apparently caused the car accident that killed his wife, an investigative reporter ends up in a small town in West Virginia when he dives into the secrets that surround the Mothman.



Documentary: Cropsey, 2009
Two documentarians are confronted with the horrifying realisation that the urban legend of their childhood, which featured a boogeyman snatching kids and taking them into the nearby abandoned hospital, actually turned out to be shockingly true.



Documentary: Killer Legends, 2014
From the same team who brought us Cropsy comes an documentary that investigates urban legends such as The Candyman, The Hookman, and the Baby Sitter and the Man Upstairs, and pinpoints the true stories that inspires them.



Podcast: Last Podcast on the Left
This horror-comedy podcast covers everything from true crime to hauntings, but their urban legend-inspired episodes are particularly notable. Listen and enjoy!



Book: The Big Book of Urban Legends
This encyclopaedia-style book includes over 200 tales of urban legends. I think it’s safe to say that their book may be a bit more in depth than our little library, but does it have the same charm? I think not.
