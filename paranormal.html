Film: The Conjuring, 2013
James Wan’s contemporary horror film follows paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren as they help a family that is being tormented by a dark entity.



Book: Haunting of Hill House by Shirley Jackson
This classic ghost story follows a researcher and three assistants as they conduct research on the paranormal by staying for a prolonged period of time at a notoriously haunted mansion. They are plagued by strange occurrences and disturbing encounters, but they soon discover that some of the worst ghosts reside within themselves.



Book: Heart Shaped Box by Joe Hill
An aging rock star who collects strange and allegedly cursed objects for his person museum of the macabre purchases a ghost over the internet, believing it to be a hoax. He could not be more wrong.



Film: Poltergeist, 1982
This classic horror film features a family being terrorized by vengeful ghosts. Warning: there is a scary clown scene, so have fun with that.



TV Series: American Horror Story: Murder House, FX
An American family moves into a new home that holds a dark past full of brutal murders only to find out that they are not the only inhabitants of the home.
